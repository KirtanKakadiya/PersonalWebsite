Hack the Change Hackathon

  • In November of 2021, I competed in a Hackathon where our team came in the top 6 and were able to win three awards (Best Use of Cloud Computing, Best Use of APIs, and Best User Experience).
  • The prompt of the Hackathon was to create a web app which related to mental health.
  • Our team decides to use React.js and Node.js on the Frontend of the App and Python on the BackEnd of the App.
  • The web app consisted of a survey which had 10 questions, using Pandas and a kNN classifer we were able to predict the user's symptoms. Depending on the result we directed the user to multiple websites which may help the user in their mental health.
